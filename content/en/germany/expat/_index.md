## Expat Guides — Processes I Actually Went Through

There's plenty of information online about moving to Germany — but most of it is either official source summaries or incomplete.

Coming from Turkey and having also obtained Bulgarian citizenship, I went through several of these processes from two different angles. The path for someone arriving with a Turkish licence is different from someone with an EU licence. The rental market treats foreigners differently depending on their paperwork. Knowing what to do when you're fired versus not knowing makes a very real difference in the outcome.

These guides exist to close that gap.
