## Living in Germany — Honest and Comparative

Moving to Germany is a decision — but the decision never really ends.

Most content about life in Germany is either a rephrasing of official sources or relentlessly positive. Coming from Turkey and settling in Munich, I've found some things genuinely better than expected — and others considerably more bureaucratic than anyone warned me about.

The writing in this section covers daily life as it actually is: what's surprisingly good, what's unexpectedly difficult, and what you'll only understand once you're already here.
