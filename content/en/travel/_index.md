## Travel Guides — Every City, Every Problem, Every Lesson

Cities and countries we've visited, and everything that happened along the way.

No "must-see" lists, no glossy highlights. Instead: why that restaurant was a disappointment, why that border crossing took three hours, whether that hotel was actually worth it. Travelling with two kids aged 1 and 5, and a large double stroller, changes how every destination feels — some cities turn out far more manageable than expected, others are harder than photos suggest.
