## Welcome to Share Hub

This is not a travel blog with glossy photos and "must-visit" lists.

It's a first-hand record written by a Network Engineer living in Munich — someone who drove across Europe with two kids and a double stroller, navigated Germany's bureaucratic maze, and explored the Balkans and the Caucasus on a budget. What you'll find here is what actually happened: the delays, the wrong turns, the unexpected wins, and the things worth knowing before you go.

Renewing a foreign driving license, finding an apartment in Munich, dealing with health insurance, understanding Jobcenter rules, buying a car without a German credit history... I've done all of it — and written it down so you don't have to figure it out from scratch.

### Why This Site?

There are plenty of travel blogs. There are plenty of Germany expat guides. But very few answer the questions that actually matter before you go: How much did it cost? How long did it take? Which office did you deal with? What would you do differently?

That's the gap this site tries to fill.
